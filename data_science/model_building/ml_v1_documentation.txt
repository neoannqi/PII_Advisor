Training Data Set 
Training data is 700 resumes, taken from Indeed.com. These are focused primarily in the technology industry.
These have been annotated to train for the following entities: 
Name, Skills, College,Degree, Graduation Year, Years of Experience, Companies worked at, Designation, Location, Email Address, Address, Can Relocate to, Rewards and Achievements, Certifications, Links, University, projects, state

Limitation of Training Data Sets
1.Training data set is not trained on a variety of (i) Names, (ii) Formats
2.Dataset is relatively small for training a resume parser.
